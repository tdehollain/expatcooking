Cornish Game Hen & Potato Sauté

By Chad

[DECENT INTRO]

Ingredients

Serving 12

Cornish Hen

  • 8 cornish game hens
  • 1 lemon, quartered
  • 4 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1/3 cup low-sodium chicken broth

Potato Sauté

  • Bag of red potatoes
  • Cream of mushroom soup
  • Parley
  • Heavy cream
  • Standard sharp cheddar and white sharp cheddar cheese
  • Flour
  • Crushed seasoned croutons

Preparation

Preparation Time: 90 min
Cooking Time: 60 min
Total Time: 150 min
