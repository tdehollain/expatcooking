Feta-Spinach Cream Sauce & Chicken

By Rob

[DECENT INTRODUCTION]

Ingredients

Serving 4
  • 500gr spaghetti
  • 4 shallots, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
  • 2 tablespoons plain flour
  • 400ml milk
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese
  • 300gr spinach, boiled and drained
  • 2 tablespoons parsley, finely chopped
  • 6 tablespoons red bell peppers, finelay chopped (capsicum)
  • 2 tablespoons margarine

Preparation

Preparation Time: 25 min
Cooking Time: 16 min
Total Time: 41 min
  1. Heat margarin in a skillet
  2. Saute shallots and garlic for 4 minutes in the margarine. Stir while doing so.
  3. Add flour
  4. Cook for 1 minute on low flame
  5. Add milk and remove from heat
  6. Keep aside
  7. Squeeze out the water from the spinach and chop
  8. Add spinach, cheese, bell pepper, salt and pepper to taste the cooked sauce
  9. Re-heat on low heat
  10. Stir well for a minute
  11. Remove from heat
  12. Garnish with parlsey
  13. Serve on pasta
